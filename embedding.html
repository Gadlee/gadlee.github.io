This mobile application is tailored for Ugandan farmers. It uses Flutter for the front-end, Firebase for backend services, and AI/ML models for intelligent forecasting and insights.

Key Features

  1. AI-Powered Market Prediction:
    • Uses historical data, real-time market prices, and global trade patterns to forecast:
      • Likely future crop prices
      • Best planting and harvesting windows
      • Regional demand projections
  2. Localized News Aggregation:
    • Automatically extracts and summarizes relevant agricultural news from:
      • Government portals (e.g., MAAIF)
      • Trade/export reports
      • Weather bulletins
      • International agriculture journals
  3. Personalized Crop Dashboard:
    • Farmers can select crops they grow (e.g., maize, coffee, beans)
    • App provides tailored insights for each selected crop, including:
      • Ideal planting time (based on market and weather trends)
      • Suggested selling time and preferred regions
      • Predicted price range (with confidence score)
  4. Push Notifications:
    • Alerts farmers of:
      • Sudden market changes (e.g., price crashes)
      • Government announcements (e.g., export bans)
  5. AI & Data Engine Behind the Scenes:
    • Models trained on price trends using:
      • UBOS (Uganda Bureau of Statistics) price reports
      • FAO and World Bank datasets
    • Uses machine learning techniques such as:
      • Time series forecasting (Prophet, LSTM)
      • Text summarization for news using NLP
      • Classification models for alert thresholds
